# Research: Deep Mentor Profiles

## Decision: Add Public Deep Identity Fields Now

**Rationale**: Rich mentor identity is core product value and can be added safely without providers or secrets.

**Alternatives considered**:

- Wait for AI generation: delays core product language.
- Keep only short personality strings: too shallow for the intended council.

## Decision: Include Strengths And Blind Spots

**Rationale**: Blind spots prevent mentor personality from becoming false authority and support epistemic honesty.

**Alternatives considered**:

- Only positive traits: simpler but less honest.
